    iMagine is a fan game inspired by Magi Nation, a Canadian anime broadcast in 2007 and 2010. The show involves the Final Dreamers Tony, Strag, and Edyn facing off against Agram and his Shadow Magi in a quest to gain all of the Dreamstones in order to lock Agram in his domain, called the Core, forever.
